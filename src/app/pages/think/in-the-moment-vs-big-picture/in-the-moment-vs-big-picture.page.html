IN-THE-MOMENT vs. BIG PICTURE

The purpose of S.O.S. is to practice aligning our In-The-Moment intentions with our Big Picture intentions. When triggered your Big Picture intentions may fly out the window. With practice you will gain awareness of habitual vs intentionalresponses.

IN-THE-MOMENT Intentions are:

  • • Situational
  • • Short term
  • • Based on immediate gratification


BIG PICTURE Intentions are:

  • • Overarching, global
  • • Values based
  • • Character based (?)
  • • Long term oriented
