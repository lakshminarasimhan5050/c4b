About Think

Think Well covers two functions of the brain.
Analytical/Executive function controls
  • High level cognitive skills and critical thinking
  • Regulation of Emotions and Mood
Emotional/Primitive function controls
  • Survival Instincts
  • Raw Emotional reactions

THINK is based on the premise that:

  • THOUGHTS create EMOTIONS which drive BEHAVIOR
    • Repeated THOUGHTS become BELIEFS
    • Repeated BEHAVIORS become HABITS
  • TRIGGERS create EMOTIONAL REACTIONS which cause a BRAIN HIJACK
    • BRAIN HIJACK happens when the emotional brain overrides the analytical mind
